# Contributing to the Open Kuyper Translation

Thank you for your interest in this project. This is an **open translation** — the entire process happens in public, and contributions from readers, scholars, and theologians are welcome.

## Translation Philosophy: "Useful and Faithful over Polished"

Our primary mandate is the preservation of Abraham Kuyper's unique intellectual and rhetorical architecture. We believe that to "polish" Kuyper into modern English is to erase the very precision and classical weight that make his work authoritative.

### The Linguistic Mandate
- **Preservation of Voice:** We do not over-modernize Kuyper's 1916 Dutch rhetorical style.
- **Architectonic Prose:** We maintain his sweeping periodic sentences, elevated classical vocabulary, and rigorous theological precision.
- **Faithfulness over Fluidity:** We prioritize the faithful rendering of his logical structures over contemporary "readability." If a sentence is long and complex in the original, it must remain so in the English—provided the logic is clear.

## How to Contribute

### 1. Report a Translation Error
Found a mistranslation, typo, or error in the English text?
→ [Open a Translation Error report](https://github.com/metcalfdaniel-dotcom/kuyper-antirevolutionary-politics/issues/new?template=translation-error.yml)

### 2. Suggest an Improvement
Have a better way to phrase something? Think a theological term should be translated differently?
→ [Open a Translation Suggestion](https://github.com/metcalfdaniel-dotcom/kuyper-antirevolutionary-politics/issues/new?template=translation-suggestion.yml)

### 3. Discuss Philosophy & Approach
Want to talk about translation philosophy, theological decisions, or the project's direction?
→ [Open a Philosophy & Approach discussion](https://github.com/metcalfdaniel-dotcom/kuyper-antirevolutionary-politics/issues/new?template=philosophy-feedback.yml)

### 4. Report a Bug
Something broken in the HTML editions, PDF exports, or build scripts?
→ [Open a Bug Report](https://github.com/metcalfdaniel-dotcom/kuyper-antirevolutionary-politics/issues/new?template=bug-report.yml)

### 5. Submit a Direct Edit (Pull Request)
If you're comfortable with Git, you can fork the repo, edit the translation files directly, and submit a pull request. See the [project README](README.md) for the file structure.

### 6. Join the Conversation
For open-ended discussions that aren't tied to a specific text change, use [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/metcalfdaniel-dotcom/kuyper-antirevolutionary-politics/discussions).

## What We're Looking For

- **Accuracy** — Does the English faithfully represent Kuyper's Dutch?
- **Theological precision** — Are key terms (Sphere Sovereignty, Common Grace, Ordinances of God) translated consistently and correctly?
- **Kuyperian voice** — Does the English capture Kuyper's authoritative, architectonic, 19th-century Reformed prose style?
- **Structural Fidelity** — Are the periodic sentences and classical cadences preserved?

## What We're NOT Looking For

- **Modernization** — Do not strip away the "elevated" nature of the prose to make it sound contemporary.
- **Condensing** — Do not summarize or condense his arguments for the sake of brevity.
- **Imposing** — Do not impose contemporary political frameworks onto his thought.
